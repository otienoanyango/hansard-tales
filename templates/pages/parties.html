About Party Data

Party affiliations are based on official records from the National Assembly. MPs may change parties during their term, and we track these changes through the mp_terms table.

Performance metrics include total statements made by party members, average statements per MP, and session attendance rates.

Note: Independent MPs (IND) are grouped together for statistical purposes but represent individual candidacies.
