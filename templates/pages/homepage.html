What is Hansard Tales?

Hansard Tales makes political accountability accessible by analyzing official parliamentary records (Hansard transcripts) and presenting MP performance data in an easy-to-understand format.

Every week, we process the latest Hansard documents from the National Assembly, identify which MPs spoke, extract their statements, and track their participation in parliamentary debates.

All data comes from official sources - every statement links back to the original Hansard PDF so you can verify the information yourself.
