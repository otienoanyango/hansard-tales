## Design Notes for Web Version

### Color Scheme
- **Header**: Deep blue (#1e3a8a) with white text
- **Good Performance**: Green (#16a34a) 
- **Concerning Issues**: Orange/Red (#dc2626)
- **Key Stats**: Gold highlights (#f59e0b)
- **Background**: Light gray (#f8fafc)

### Visual Elements
- **Icons**: Money bags, school buildings, hospital symbols
- **Charts**: Simple bar charts showing price comparisons
- **Progress Bars**: MP performance scores with color coding
- **Kenyan Context**: Use recognizable imagery (maize, tea, matatu)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, large fonts
- **Stats**: Extra large, colored numbers
- **Quotes**: Italicized with speech bubble design
- **MP Names**: Bold with party colors

### Mobile Optimization
- **Two-column layout** becomes single-column on mobile
- **Expandable sections** for detailed information
- **Swipeable carousel** for multiple infographics
- **Large touch targets** for social sharing

### Kenyan Cultural Context
- **Currency**: All amounts in KSh with clear explanations
- **Equivalences**: Schools, teachers, maize, sugar, milk (relatable items)
- **Language**: Mix of English with occasional Kiswahili phrases
- **References**: Kenyan context (matatu, boda boda, etc.)
